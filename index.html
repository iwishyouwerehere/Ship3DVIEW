Ship Designer

LICENSELESS 3D MODELLING

Let your creativity flow and share models easily. Ship Designer - 3D Share module is a free service that allows you to open any 3D Model file (3dm, igs, stp, catia and more..) directly in your browser licenseless, explore the model and then export it to any extension you want or share it.

OPEN YOUR MODEL NOW!

OPEN 3D MODEL

ShipDesigner allows you to open any 3D File extension directly on our server. It does not require you to have a license, simply drag and drop your file

VIEW 3D MODEL

ShipDesigner will allow you to view the model and mantain the original file properties in a licenseless environment for free without having to buy a license for every software

EDIT 3D MODEL

ShipDesigner allows you to edit your 3D Model, adding dimensions or notes, hiding parts, deleting surfaces and anything that you'd be able to do on your favorite software

SHARE & EXPORT

ShipDesigner allows you to export the model to any 3D file Extension and download it for free, or alternatively you can share the model with a simple link !

SHARE YOUR IDEAS EASILY

What are you waiting for? Share your ideas easily using Ship Designer, easily add annotations to 3d models or export 3D Models to your favorite format. Open Rhinoceros, Catia, Step files online ! All extensions are supported.

FREE TO USE

Ship Designer is open source and free to use.
Ship Designer was developed with needs in mind, we needed to easily share models with our shareholders, and our shareholders needed to open Rhinoceros, Catia and STEP files online, so we created this web-based licenseless solution for free.
